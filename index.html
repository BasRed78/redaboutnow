About

The short version

Half British, half Dutch. Grew up between two countries and cultures that approach problems very differently - one apologises before giving feedback, the other gives it without waiting for permission. I've learned to do both.

My career looks like someone couldn't make up their mind, but there's a thread: I like exploring new or complex situations where the brand, marketing or teams need work. Telecoms, luxury design, sustainable construction, emerging tech.

Each time, the job was the same: cut through complexity, help teams move with focus. Whether that's defining positioning, leading through change or building a marketing function from scratch. Often when things are moving fast, not quite clear or too important to get wrong. I bring structure where there's chaos, creative thinking where there's noise, and calm momentum when everyone's spinning.

I build things now too. Partly because I can, partly because I've seen gaps that annoyed me enough to do something about them. GOOS and SEEN both came from personal experience. Billy came from watching my father struggle with the consequences of what happened to him.

Based in Amsterdam. London and LA feel like second homes. Happiest when I'm slightly out of my depth and working with people who care more about getting it right than getting credit.
